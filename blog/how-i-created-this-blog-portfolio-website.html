How I Created This Blog/Portfolio Website

August 14, 2016
Tutorials

Background

This was a project that I started on 28 December, 2020. Prior to starting this project, I was applying for jobs and going to interviews despite having a job already. My job then was more of a technical support role so I was desperate to change jobs and do actual development, in spite of the job being really good in terms of compensation as well as having a great boss, colleagues and working environment in general. I was job searching for about a month and a half, and it was really tough getting no-calls. I think it was due to a combination of a lack of professional dev experience, insufficient interview preparation, a shunning of professional networking and a higher-than-average salary for my work experience bracket.

Fortunately, I did end up getting a job for a previous employer as a backend / data engineer and instructional associate. It’s a lot of hats to wear and they offered lower compensation but hey, it’s a startup and I’m young enough to take risks like that. I was optimizing for learning anyway and the learning environment there is second to none after interviewing for a ton of companies.

After stumbling upon this article and not wanting to experience a high level of difficulty in my next job search, I decided to up my online networking game and increase my social presence. Given my personality, I believed that Step 1 was to create an online portfolio and start a blog. Hence, this website was born. But enough about me, let’s get on to the technical stuff!
